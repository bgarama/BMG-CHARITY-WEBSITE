Zero Plastic Football Fiesta 2025

Sports for Environment, Youth Engagement and Zero Plastic Awareness

Date: December 2025 | Location: Mnarani, Kilifi County

Using Football to Champion Environmental Action

BAHA MADZO GADZE FOR CHARITY organized the Zero Plastic Football Fiesta 2025 as a powerful sports-for-environment initiative bringing together youth, community members, partners, and football teams to promote environmental awareness and action.

The event combined football, plastic pollution awareness, cleanup action, teamwork, and community mobilization. It demonstrated how sports can be used as a strong platform to educate young people and inspire collective responsibility toward a cleaner Kilifi.

Through this initiative, BAHA MADZO GADZE FOR CHARITY continued strengthening its Zero Plastic Era Campaign by linking youth talent, environmental responsibility, and community participation.

We sincerely appreciate all participating teams, volunteers, partners, community members, and supporters who contributed to making the Zero Plastic Football Fiesta 2025 a successful and impactful event.

Key Focus Areas

  • Environmental awareness through sports
  • Youth engagement and talent development
  • Plastic pollution education
  • Community cleanup and responsibility
  • Partnership and grassroots mobilization
