About the Initiative

The BAHA Digital Innovation Hub is an upcoming social enterprise initiative of BAHA MADZO GADZE FOR CHARITY that seeks to bridge technology, innovation, and sustainable community development.

The hub is being established to provide digital services, technology solutions, and skills development opportunities while generating sustainable income to support the organization's environmental programmes.

By creating an internal digital ecosystem, we aim to reduce dependency on donations and build long-term sustainability for our conservation efforts.

Why We Are Building This Hub

Tree Planting

Revenue generated by the hub will support tree planting and ecosystem restoration projects.

Waste Management

Supporting waste segregation, recycling, and environmental cleanup activities.

Climate Action
